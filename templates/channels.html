How to Add Channels

Single Channel
  1. Find the channel you want to monitor
  2. Copy the username (the part after "t.me/" in the channel link)
  3. Paste it in the form above (without the @ symbol)
  4. Click "Add Channel"
Multiple Channels
  1. Collect usernames of multiple channels you want to monitor
  2. Enter them in the text area, separated by commas or one per line
  3. Make sure to remove the @ symbol from all usernames
  4. Click "Add All Channels"
Note: The bot must be a member of the channels to extract links.
